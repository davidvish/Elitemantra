About Us

Our team here to support the Indians currently based all over the world, with their undertakings in India.

Our team is of experienced and adept Advocates, Real Estate Advisors, Chartered Accountants, Investigators and Manpower Services, having multiple years’ experience in their respective fields. We have joined hands to assist Indians based abroad in requirements pertaining (but not limited) to Property issues, Legal Issues, Wealth managing issues,  Investigation, Taxation etc

  • Legal services
  • Financial & Wealth Management
  • Real Estate Advisory
  • Investigation and Detective services
  • Documentation

     Mission

  • We assist NRIs from various countries to achieve short term aims while based at the comfort of their homes by offering beyond traditional prosperity management.
  • Our mission is to ensure ease and comfort for pour clientele with our objective-oriented approach and focus on client needs.

      Vision

  • We aim to be a leading office to support NRIs in India, providing all kind of solutions for their prosperity.
  • Our goal is to provide all kind of support to the families residing out of India for the purpose of employment or livelihood.

Our services for NRI families and their offices are a mirroring of our expertise. We are giving commitment towards family businesses around the world.
Our overall approach helps families to form their prosperity and maintain it for future generations. Our goal is to unseal the developing future of legal, real estate, financial & wealth management, investigation, and documentation related aspects.
